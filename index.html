About Me

Hi, I'm Adam, an amateur portrait artist with a passion for capturing the character, emotion and integrity of a subject.

During the day, I work as a legal librarian at a top US law firm in London, at night I paint portraits.

Portrait painting has always been a hobby of mine. Most of my work to date has involved painting family members and friends - now I would like to branch out and offer a low rate commission service to the wider public. I don't have the luxury of an artistic studio, so all of my creations are produced within a dedicated space on my kitchen table.

Artistic Medium

As an amateur artist, I have experimented using a range of artistic mediums, namely acrylic, oil and watercolour painting, as well as oil pastel and charcoal.

Acrylic paints and oil pastels are my preferred mediums, so my work focuses on using these. The latter medium helps to enhance tones and the notable features of the subject.
